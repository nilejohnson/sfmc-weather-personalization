DYNAMIC SUBJECT LINE (paste in Email Subject field):
====================================================

%%=v(@subjectLine)=%%


EXAMPLES BY WEATHER:
--------------------
Sunny:  "Perfect weather in Sydney! Time to get outside"
Rainy:  "Rain in Sydney? We've got you covered!"
Cold:   "Cold week ahead in Reykjavik - Stay warm!"
Snowy:  "Snow in Svalbard! Winter magic is here"
Cloudy: "This week's best deals for you in Tokyo"

PREHEADER EXAMPLES (includes 7-day average):
-------------------------------------------
Sunny:  "It's 28°C today, averaging 28°C this week - shop summer essentials!"
Rainy:  "Week ahead averaging 20°C - stay dry with our rainy day picks!"
Cold:   "Averaging 0°C this week. Bundle up with our winter essentials!"
Snowy:  "Week ahead averaging -19°C - embrace the winter wonderland!"
Cloudy: "Currently 12°C, weekly average 12°C - check out our picks!"


NOTE: The @subjectLine variable is set in the email body AMPscript.
Make sure the AMPscript block runs BEFORE the subject line is evaluated
by placing it at the very top of your email HTML.
